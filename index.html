About Palki

Palki Sharma Upadhyay is an award-winning Indian journalist and news anchor currently serving as the Editor-in-Chief of Firstpost. Known for her sharp analysis and incisive questioning, she has become one of India's most recognizable faces in global news coverage.

Born on May 29, 1982, Palki holds a Master's degree in Communication and Journalism from the Indian Institute of Mass Communication (IIMC), Delhi. She also has a degree in design, specializing in ceramics and interior design.

Her career spans over two decades across major news networks including Doordarshan, CNN-IBN, WION, and now Firstpost. She is particularly known for her ability to explain complex international issues in simple terms.
