About Palki Sharma

Journalist | News Anchor | Managing Editor

Born on May 29, 1982, in India, Palki Sharma holds a Master’s in Communication and Journalism from IHC (IIS), Delhi University, and a degree in design. She began her career at Doordarshan in 2002 and rose to prominence through CNN-IBN, WION, and now Firstpost.

Early Career (2002-2005)

News anchor at Doordarshan, covering daily bulletins and interviews.

CNN-IBN (2005-2015)

Prime-time anchor and journalist, covering politics and business.

WION (2016-2022)

Managing Editor and host of "Gravitas," gaining global recognition.

Firstpost (2023-Present)

Host of "Vantage with Palki Sharma," continuing her sharp global analysis.
